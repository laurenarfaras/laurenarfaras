hello

I'm Lauren Arfaras! I am a computer science student at the University of Florida with a passion for being creative. My code is my craft. I love building projects that both function well and look great.

When I first started computer science, it was not what I expected at all, I was frustrated that my projects were all command line programs that had a horrible user interface. So I took a class in full-stack web development and loved it. I could create projects that were powerful and ran on a browser! This brought me so much more creative freedom and excitement towards my work.

When I am not coding you can find me snuggling my cat + pup, hand-lettering, or hanging out with some friends.

I hope to always be learning, creating, and impacting the lives of others. If you want to work together, don't hesitate to reach out! I would love to create something beautiful with you.
